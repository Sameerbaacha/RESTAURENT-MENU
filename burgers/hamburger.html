Hamburger




ingredients:

- 1 pound ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat is recommended)
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 4 hamburger buns
- 4 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, Swiss, or your favorite)
- Optional toppings: Lettuce, tomato slices, onion slices, pickles.
- ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise
