beef nihary platter




Ingredients:

-1 kg beef stew meat, preferably shank or other cuts with bone
-1/2 cup ghee or oil
-2 large onions, finely sliced
-4 cloves garlic, minced
-1-inch piece of ginger, grated
-2 tablespoons nihari masala (available in stores or make your own blend)
-1 teaspoon turmeric powder
-1 teaspoon coriander powder
-1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
-1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
-Salt to taste
-8 cups water
-4 tablespoons wheat flour (optional, for thickening)
-Fresh coriander leaves and sliced ginger for garnish
